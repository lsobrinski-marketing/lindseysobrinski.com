Paste your domain. This analyzer reads your homepage, robots.txt, and llms.txt, then samples a few interior pages from your sitemap — because a homepage alone doesn't tell the whole story. It checks what ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity can actually see and cite. Most sites fail at least one check they've never heard of.

Fix first

    Runs entirely in your browser; no data is stored. Samples your homepage plus a few pages from your sitemap via a fetch proxy, so a few sites will refuse the request. Answer-content checks (FAQ, question-and-answer structure) are judged on content pages, not your homepage. This is the on-site half of GEO; the off-site half (whether third parties cite you) needs a query audit per engine.
