Each engine plays a different position in the journey

One "AI SEO playbook" is a mistake. Map the play to where each engine sits.

Awareness · Top of Funnel

ChatGPT

Your awareness layer. Rewards citations and third-party corroboration. When someone asks a big open question about your category, this is where your brand gets introduced. Think impressions, not clicks.

Consideration · Mid / Bottom Funnel

Gemini + AI Overviews

Closer to the money. Reward entity authority and surface brand mentions when buyers are comparing, shortlisting, and deciding. Consideration is happening inside the answer box.

Research · Validation

Perplexity

The researcher's engine. Dense citations, sources on display. If your expertise is locked in gated PDFs and webinars, it does not exist here.
