About

I lead performance marketing, SEO/AEO/GEO, and marketing analytics for a 9-figure multi-brand retailer, where I sit on the Center of Excellence board and submit weekly forecasts to PE governance. I built the AI-search function from a standing start: the brand is now cited across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude, and earned-media mentions grew 3.6x.

Before that I was Acting VP of Marketing at a $40M B2B services company, where I built the lead-generation and lifecycle engine (6sense, Pardot, Salesforce, HubSpot) that added $5M in revenue, and separately led the turnaround of a subscription DTC brand and managed paid media for eleven DTC beauty and retail brands on Shopify Plus, including luxury names.

I am AI-native in the practical sense: I build the agents that run my reporting, forecasting, and media-mix modeling, with a human on top setting strategy and validating the decision chain. My work is a featured case study in Mark Jeffery's forthcoming book on AI-driven marketing. 15+ years in. The measurement is the point.
