About

I am a growth and performance marketing leader with 15+ years across enterprise retail, B2B services, agencies, and subscription DTC. My work lives at the intersection of media, organic search, and measurement: building the channel mix, building the AI-search visibility engines that get brands cited across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude, and building the analytics that tell you what actually worked.

I have led marketing as Acting VP at a $40M B2B services company, where the demand engine I built added $5M in revenue and grew margin from 13% to 35%. Today I lead performance marketing, SEO/AEO/GEO, and marketing analytics for a national retail brand.

I am AI-native in the practical sense: I build the agents that run my reporting, forecasting, and media-mix modeling, with a human on top setting strategy and validating the decision chain. My work is a featured case study in Mark Jeffery's forthcoming book on AI-driven marketing. The measurement is the point.
